Garment Printing Club was a student-led screenprinting workshop and exhibition program. I coordinated weekly instruction, prepared artwork for print, maintained supplies, and managed purchasing and budgets.

The club supported first-time printers alongside experienced members, organized three exhibitions, and produced printed material for student organizations and community fundraising.
