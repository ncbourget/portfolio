ReRun Film Festival

Branding • Motion • Publication

Spring 2026

01 Festival ephemera 02 Selected spreads

ReRun Film Festival is a visual system for a film festival built around replay, circulation, and the strange afterlife of moving images. The work moves between identity, motion language, and printed programming.

The system uses repeated frames, bold festival graphics, and publication assets to make the event feel active before, during, and after the screening space.
