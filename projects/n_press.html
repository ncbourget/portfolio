ñ press

Leadership • Team-building • Publication • Graphics

April 2025—present

01 ñ Open Studio Days 02 ICA Watershed 03 Silkscreen Night 04 Graphic Work 05 Multiple Formats 06 Wheatpasting

ñ press is a publishing and print initiative organized around shared tools, collective production, and the social life of printed matter. The identity behaves like the workshop itself: direct, changeable, practical, and open to evidence of the hand.

The system moves between publications, screenprinted garments, workshop materials, and events. Rather than smoothing the work into a single polished voice, it gives color, overprinting, repetition, and imperfect registration room to become part of the language.
