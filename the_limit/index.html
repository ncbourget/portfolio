The Limit

Interactive • Research • Publication • Web

Spring 2026

01 Manual 02 Dream Chatroom 03 Pretty Lag Machine

The Limit is a senior thesis project about how graphical constraint can make digital spaces feel more intense, unfinished, and emotionally charged. The project quickly became a cross-section of the modern low-poly 3D community and the ethos surrounding it.

The final deliverables ended up as an ambient experience (Pretty Lag Machine), an active experience (Dream Chatroom), and a 126-page digital reflection book with 20 interviews from notable members of the community. Although developed as a senior thesis, the programs are still receiving updates.
