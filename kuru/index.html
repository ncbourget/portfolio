KURU Fine Dining

Branding • Craft

Spring 2026

01 Brand guidelines 02 Menus

KURU Fine Dining is a restaurant identity system built around a deliberately crafted dining experience. The work includes brand guidelines, menu systems, and printed ephemera.

The project balances a polished fine-dining voice with tactile details: typography, menu hierarchy, and small printed moments that make the brand feel handled rather than only displayed.
