ReRun Film Festival

Branding • Motion • Publication

Spring 2026

01 Festival ephemera 02 Selected spreads

ReRun Film Festival is an identity system for films that transform on a second viewing, where meaning shifts once the audience knows what they have seen.

The project includes tickets, an ID card, a 28-page program, and a motion bumper, building one festival language across print and time-based media.

VHS timelines, rewind iconography, and Lineto Replica shape the system, using repetition and playback as both visual language and structure.
