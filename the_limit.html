The Limit

Interactive • Research • Publication • Web

Spring 2026

01 Manual 02 Dream Chatroom 03 Pretty Lag Machine

The Limit is a senior thesis project about how graphical constraint can make digital spaces feel more intense, unfinished, and emotionally charged. The work moves between a manual, interviews, and small browser-based environments that treat limitation as a material.

Dream Chatroom and Pretty Lag Machine use friction, low-resolution charm, and deliberate technical obstruction as part of the experience. Instead of optimizing away the awkwardness, the project lets lag, abstraction, and incomplete worlds become the point.
