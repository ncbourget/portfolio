CRUSHED! Bike grips

Branding • Fabrication

Spring 2026

01 3D Model 02 Product shots

CRUSHED! Bike grips is a product and identity study for custom bicycle grips. The project combines branding, object fabrication, and digital presentation into a small but complete product system.

The grip is shown as both a physical object and a rotating model, allowing the form, texture, and attitude of the piece to carry the identity beyond a flat logo treatment.
