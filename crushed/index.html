CRUSHED! Bike grips

Branding • Fabrication

Spring 2026

01 3D Model 02 Product shots

CRUSHED! is a packaging and product design project for 3D-printed bicycle grips made with flexible neon green TPU filament.

Extruded skulls sit beneath the rider’s fingers and collapse under pressure, turning the grip into a tactile reminder that your life is literally in your hands.

The project pulls from grunge, mall skater, and early MTV aesthetics. Hand-screenprinted packaging uses glow-in-the-dark ink, while a modified version of Opium gives the identity an unstable, bone-like structure.
