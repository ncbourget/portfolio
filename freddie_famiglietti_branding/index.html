Freddie Famiglietti is a Boston-based oil painter and one of my closest friends. The project gives his website the kind of old-soul atmosphere that radiates off of him.

I drew from old Web 1.0 sites for restorative sculptors, house painters, and people working between art and labor, aiming for something sophisticated, handmade, and slightly late-90s.
