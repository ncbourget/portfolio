Amelia Zine is a love letter to Amelia, a typeface developed by Stan Davis in 1967 and later used across retrofuturistic and psychedelic films and book covers.

The publication uses the typeface to imagine guidelines for humans moving to Mars, treating typographic history as a way to build a strange instructional future.
