NATE BOURGET

3D Work

Animation • Modeling

Summer 2024—present

01 Animation studies 02 Models 03 Installation

My work in 3D has been mostly utilitarian, focused on quick render speeds, simplified forms, and the specific atmosphere of low-resolution texture.

These studies connect to the thinking behind The Limit, where graphical constraint becomes a material rather than a compromise. I am most interested in the nuance of making simple characters feel specific, strange, and alive.

Working in Blender has also become a practical foundation for motion graphics, giving me a faster way to think through timing, lighting, camera movement, and animation systems.
